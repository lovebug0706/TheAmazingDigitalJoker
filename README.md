# The Amazing Digital Joker (temp name)
A Balatro mod based on The Amazing Digital Circus, a show by Glitch and Gooseworx.

## About
I recently got into TADC, and realized there wasn't a specific mod for this series. There were jokers referencing this series sprinkled throughout other mods, but no specific mod dedicated towards the show, so I decided to make it myself.

## Cards
### Kinger (Legendary)
One of the only legendary cards currently.

Gives X2.5 Mult, and gains x0.75 mult each round. Also has another perk, but also is a spoiler.
<details>
  <summary>Episode 8 spoiler.</summary>
If placed in the same layout as Caine, Caine will get deleted.
</details>

### Caine (Rare)
Knocked down to Rare for now.

Gives 5X Mult, and gains x1 mult each round, there are disadvantages however.

Has a 1/6 chance to remove another joker (and gain x0.5 mult), and a 1/3 chance to spawn Bubble.

### Bubble (Common)
The TADC equivalent to the Yahimod Horse.

Gives x0.8 Mult and x0.8 Chips, is removable in 2 rounds.

## Card Types
### Abstraction Cards
Gives 2.5x mult, is it's own rank and suit, and has 1/4 chance to be sent to the cellar / be destroyed upon scoring.
### Disappearing Cards
Gives 1.1x chips, but has a 1/10 chance to disappear / get destroyed when clicked on.

## Tarot Cards
### The Cellar
Turns 3 cards into Abstraction Cards.
### National Anthem
Turns 3 cards into Disappearing Cards.
